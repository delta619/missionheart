About Us

Welcome to Mission Heart Foundation Since 2019

We are a non profit organisation working towards curing congenital heart diseases in children across India.

Congenital heart disease (CHD) is one of the most common contributing factors towards childhood morbidity and mortality. In India, approximately 3,00,000 babies are born every year with some form of CHD. Of these, 1,00,000 require surgical or trans catheter intervention to alleviate their symptoms or prolong their life or both.

Mission Heart Foundation supports the cardiac treatment of pediatric patients with Coronary Heart Disease, this will include device closures as well as surgical repairs. We provide funding for the treatment for the financially challenged girl child. We will begin with covering children from Mumbai and its suburbs Navi Mumbai and plan to expand the activity to other parts of Maharashtra.

Founding Members

Dr Robin Pinto

Is an eminent cardiologist with a vast experience of over 30 years in the field. He has a special interest in pediatric cardiology and has authored numerous national and international articles in the same. Dr Pinto has always felt strongly about providing quality care and financial support for the girl child with congenital heart disease as he is of the firm belief that these children do not obtain optimum care in India for a variety of social reasons.

Cherylann Pinto

Is an Executive Director in a pharmaceutical company.  Having the scientific & CSR background, she is passionate about giving back to society and improving lives of the lesser priviledged children & families in Maharashtra.  Building awareness and helping parents better understand the ailment and care required for Congenital Heart Disease is one of the objectives which she intends to drive. She has been recognized for her contribution to CSR in Asia and her personal dream is to build Mission Heart foundation to help patients in need.
